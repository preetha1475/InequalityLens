📘 About the Dataset

This dataset contains global economic indicators including GDP, Gini Index, poverty rate, unemployment, and income distribution metrics.

📊 Column Insights

Country

Full name of the country.

Year

Year in which the data was recorded.

Population

Total population of the country.

GDP per Capita (PPP)

Average income per person adjusted for purchasing power parity.

Gini Index

Measures income inequality (0 = perfect equality, 1 = maximum inequality).

Bottom 40% Share

Income share held by the bottom 40% of the population.

Top 10% Share

Income share held by the richest 10%.

Palma Ratio

Ratio of income share of top 10% to bottom 40% (higher = more inequality).

Poverty Rate

Percentage of people living below the poverty line.

Unemployment Rate

Percentage of workforce that is unemployed.
